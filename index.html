    About Us

    Webin-AR is a responsive web application which will revolutionize the modern way of learning with visual concepts and AR. The application will be made accessible for Pre-School kids to Higher Secondary Level Education.

    Webin-AR helps Students and Teachers in a lot of ways from improving and understanding concepts to having Interactive Virtual Live Classes. The Platform allows teachers to unlock exercises for Students after the taught sessions.
